SUDOKU

Rules & Controls

RULES

  • Fill the 9×9 grid so every row, column, and 3×3 box contains digits 1–9
  • No digit may repeat in any row, column, or box
  • Pre-filled numbers cannot be changed

CONTROLS

  • Click an empty cell to select it
  • 1–9 or numpad to enter a digit
  • Delete or 0 to clear a cell
