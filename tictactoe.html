TIC TAC TOE

Rules & Controls

CLASSIC MODE

  • Players take turns placing X or O on a 3×3 grid
  • First to get 3 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins
  • If all 9 cells are filled with no winner, it's a draw

MOVE VARIANT

  • Each player places 3 pieces, then moves them on subsequent turns
  • Move any of your pieces to an empty cell to get 3 in a row

CONTROLS

  • Click any empty cell to place or move a piece
