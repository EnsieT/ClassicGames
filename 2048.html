2048

Rules & Controls

RULES

  • Slide tiles in any direction to merge matching numbers
  • Two tiles with the same number merge into one (e.g. 2+2=4)
  • Reach the 2048 tile to win — keep going for a higher score!
  • Game over when no more moves are possible

CONTROLS

  • ← ↑ ↓ → Slide tiles
  • Swipe on mobile/touch devices
