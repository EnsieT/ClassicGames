CHECKERS

Rules & Controls

RULES

  • Move pieces diagonally forward one square
  • Jump over opponent pieces to capture them (mandatory)
  • Chain multiple jumps in a single turn when possible
  • Reach the opposite end to become a King (moves backward too)
  • Win by capturing all opponent pieces or blocking all moves

CONTROLS

  • Click a piece to select it
  • Click a highlighted square to move
