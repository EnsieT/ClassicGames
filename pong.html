Rules & Controls

RULES

  • Score by getting the ball past your opponent's paddle
  • Ball speeds up after each hit
  • First player to reach the score limit wins

CONTROLS

  • W / S — Player 1 (left paddle)
  • ↑ / ↓ — Player 2 (right paddle)
  • Enter or Click Start   P Pause
