JIGSAW

Rules & Controls

HOW TO PLAY

  • Click or tap a puzzle piece to select it (highlighted in blue)
  • Click or tap a second piece to swap them
  • Arrange all pieces to recreate the original picture
  • Pieces in the correct position get a subtle green border

FEATURES

  • Choose a picture from the dropdown
  • Pick difficulty: Easy (3×3), Medium (4×4), or Hard (5×5)
  • Use Preview to peek at the completed image
  • Use New Game to shuffle and start over
