CONNECT FOUR

Rules & Controls

RULES

  • Players take turns dropping colored discs into columns
  • Discs fall to the lowest available slot in that column
  • First to connect 4 in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — wins

CONTROLS

  • Click a column to drop your disc
